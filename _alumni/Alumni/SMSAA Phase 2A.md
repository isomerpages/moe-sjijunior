<h3>2024 Primary One Registration Exercise Phase 2A</h3>
<p>2024 Primary One Registration Exercise Phase 2A is for</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Singapore Citizen or Singapore Permanent Resident children born between
02 January 2018 to 01 January 2019 and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>whose parent/older sibling was a former student of St Michael's School
(SMS) now known as St Joseph's Institution Junior (SJIJ)</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>If you meet the above criteria and wish to register your child during
2024 Primary One Registration Exercise Phase 2A, please click this <a href="https://form.gov.sg/641ff79372513d00123fb607" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">link</a>to
complete the form. You will need to attach a copy of the following document
as proof of affiliation.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>If the parent was a former student of SMS/SJIJ, attach the front and back
copy of parent’s NRIC</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If sibling was a former student of SMS/SJIJ, attach the birth certificate
of sibling</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Birth certificate of the child registering for P1</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Upon verification, we will prepare an eligibility letter for Phase 2A
and send it to your email.</p>
