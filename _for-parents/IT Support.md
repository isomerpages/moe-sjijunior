### **Proactive Tip: SLS Self-Reset**

To regain access to SLS instantly without waiting for support, we highly recommend that students set up **Security Questions** within their SLS profile.

*   **The Benefit:** Once configured, students can perform a **self-reset** of their password at any time.
    
*   **How to set it up:** Log in to SLS, navigate to **Profile Settings**, and update the security questions section.

https://www.learning.moe.edu.sg/login-troubleshooting/authentication/reset-sls-password-student/
